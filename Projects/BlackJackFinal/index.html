Blackjack Rules

Objective

Beat the dealer's hand without going over 21.

Card Values

  • Face cards (Jack, Queen, King) = 10
  • Ace = 1 or 11
  • All other cards = face value

Gameplay

  1. Place your bet
  2. Receive two cards
  3. Choose to Hit or Stand
  4. Try to get closer to 21 than the dealer
